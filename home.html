Introducing the Scottish Enterprise account

A personal account space for you and your business. Sign in or create an account Link to sign in.to get started.

Who and what is the account for?

For now, our account space is for invited customers only. This is so we can test the account concept and functionality with a small number of users before opening the service up to our full customer base.

If you’ve been invited to take part in the test phase of our account space, you can sign in or create an account now. As a valued customer, your experience and feedback will help us build a better service for Scottish businesses and entrepreneurs.

Need help creating your account?

We’re working on improving the sign up process. If you’re struggling to create your account in the meantime, just get in touch with your Scottish Enterprise contact. You can also speak to our enquiry team.
