What you can expect from us

Tailored support

During the first 3 months of our engagement your Account Manager will undertake a strategic diagnostic review with your senior team to get a better understanding of your business and forward strategy for growth. This allows us to identify key areas and opportunities where we can best support your business growth.

Our support can be both financial and advisory, and is largely focused on:

  • Investment funding
  • Leadership and management development
  • Sales growth
  • International expansion

We offer you access to support and expertise from specialists across Scottish Enterprise and Scottish Development International.

Access to networks and ecosystems

Scottish Enterprise works collaboratively with an extensive international network of partners and stakeholders. We'll help you make the right connections at the right time, with access to our networks of business support ecosystems and investment ecosystems in Scotland and beyond.

What we expect from you

Commercial vision and drive

We expect to see:

  • A CEO and leadership team that is driven by a big vision, big aspiration and a burning desire and ability to grow.
  • A board of directors committed to working with Scottish Enterprise, and who naturally sees itself as jointly involved in making a success of your business.
  • A willingness to build a commercially experienced management team.
  • A willingness to raise the appropriate amount of funding required to achieve this growth.

Company information

We need you to periodically and annually provide financial information, including:

  • Management accounts
  • Audited accounts
  • Turnover figured
  • Export sales figures
  • Employment data

At the end of our relationship, we'll continue to ask for basic financial information for a period of up to 3 years. This helps us monitor the long-term impact of our support.

Evidence of spend

Once a project is complete, whether or not a grant is provided, we'll ask for your input to evaluate the impact of our support on your business.

Where financial support is provided, you'll need to provide accurate evidence related to your spend. These need to be provided on time to allow payments to be made promptly.
